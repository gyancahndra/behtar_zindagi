Behtar Zindagi is a two-way marketplace that offers a data-driven solution for all farming needs by bringing a large number of agri-stakeholders into a ‘single-window’. With a future-ready transparent tech platform, it attracts the key agri-sellers, advisory experts, tools and machinery manufacturers, financial institutions, and innovative startups, to come under a single-window for solving all farming needs.”

In other words, Behtar Zindagi is a technology platform that offers:

  • Discovery of all farming needs (bringing the widest selection);
  • Accessibility of what they discover; (last mile, digital reach)
  • Return on investment (financially rewarding).
